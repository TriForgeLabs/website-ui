Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 22, 2025

At TriForgeLabs, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our web and mobile applications.

Information We Collect
  • Personal information you provide (such as name, email address, etc.)
  • Usage data and analytics
  • Device and log information
How We Use Your Information
  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To improve user experience and our products
  • To communicate with you about updates or support
  • To comply with legal obligations
Advertising

Some of our applications may display advertisements to users who do not have an active subscription. These ads may be provided by third-party advertising partners who may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar technologies to deliver relevant ads and measure their effectiveness. Information collected for advertising purposes may include device information, usage data, and, in some cases, limited demographic information. We do not share your personal information such as your name or email address with advertisers without your consent. You can manage your ad preferences or opt out of personalized ads through your device settings or by following instructions provided within the application.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our services, as required by law, or to protect our rights.

Your Choices

You may access, update, or delete your personal information by contacting us. You may also opt out of certain communications at any time.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at triforgelabs@gmail.com.

Data Retention and Account Deletion

TriForgeLabs applications only retain the minimum data necessary for user accounts. This data may include, but is not limited to, user's names, emails, account identifiers, and any provided anthropometric information.

All of the user's data is removed if the user account is deleted. If you wish to delete your account, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the application on your mobile device.
  2. Navigate to the Settings page.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm in the popup that you would like to delete the account.

Important: When an account is deleted, all data pertaining to that account is purged. Deleting an account is permanent and cannot be undone or recovered after deletion.
